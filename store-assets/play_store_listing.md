## Privacy Policy  *(required by Play Store — host this at a public URL)*

```
Privacy Policy — OoruMitra

Last updated: June 2026

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
   - Mobile phone number (for OTP authentication)
   - Name, village, and profile details you provide
   - Device location (to show nearby services — only when app is in use)
   - FCM device token (for push notifications)
   - Photos you upload for product or service listings

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
   - To verify your identity via OTP
   - To show your listings and profile to other users in your area
   - To send booking and message notifications
   - We do not sell your data to any third party

3. DATA STORAGE
   - Data is stored on secure cloud servers (AWS)
   - OTP codes are deleted after 10 minutes
   - You can request account deletion by contacting support

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
   - MSG91 (OTP delivery)
   - Firebase (push notifications, analytics)
   - Google Maps (location services)
   - AWS S3 (media storage)

5. CONTACT
   gmadan.tech@gmail.com
```
